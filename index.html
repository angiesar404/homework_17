ABOUT ME

I am a User Experience (UX) Researcher, Strategist, Designer and creative problem-solver. My background is rooted in qualitative and quantitative consumer market research and I have spent years discovering the "why" behind consumer attitudes and behaviors. I'm naturally curious and enjoy deconstructing or constructing "truths" about consumer and user motivations. I have parlayed my experience in consumer research into an expanded toolkit that includes user centered design based thinking and execution.

HOW I WORK

  • I collaboratively sketch, brainstorm, and co-create with users. I facilitate conversations, solve difficult problems, manage project details, and work closely with my team.
  • I love design research: the looking, listening, learning, and finding order in chaos.
  • I use visualization to model ecosystems, communicate research findings, and better understand complex relationships.
